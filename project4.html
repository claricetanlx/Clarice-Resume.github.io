Project Overview

A 3-month academic project designing event advertising materials for a fictional brand, Frozzi. The goal was to create a cohesive visual campaign, including posters, social media assets, and digital ads, while applying principles of branding, typography, and layout design learned in class.

Key Features

Vibrant Visual Identity

Developed bold, high-impact designs that reflected Frozzi’s lively brand persona

Cross-Platform Adaptability

Created versatile assets optimized for social media, posters, and email campaigns

Audience-Centric Design

Tailored visuals to resonate with Frozzi’s target demographic, using trends in color, typography, and imagery.Tailored visuals to resonate with Frozzi’s target demographic, using trends in color, typography, and imagery.


Challenges & Solutions

The project required creating unique designs within the constraints of a fictional brand. To stand out, I experimented with unconventional color palettes and dynamic typography, ensuring alignment with Frozzi’s playful identity. Tight deadlines were managed by prioritizing key assets and reusing design elements efficiently.

Another challenge was adapting designs across different formats (e.g., posters vs. Instagram stories). I addressed this by designing scalable templates and maintaining consistent branding. Technical hurdles, like mastering Illustrator’s pen tool, were overcome through online tutorials and practice.

Technologies Used

Adobe Illustrator Canva

  • ✅ Improved technical skills in digital illustration and layout composition
