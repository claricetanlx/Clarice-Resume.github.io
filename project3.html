Role
Graphic Designer
Duration
3 months
Year

Project Overview

A 3-month creative project for Disney, blending corporate branding with magazine design. This involved crafting visually compelling layouts that align with Disney’s magical aesthetic while maintaining professional appeal for a corporate audience. The goal was to merge storytelling with brand consistency across print and digital mediums.

Key Features

Brand-Aligned Design

Created cohesive visuals that reflect Disney’s iconic style while adapting to corporate and editorial needs

Multimedia Integration

Designed for seamless transition between print magazines and digital platforms


Challenges & Solutions

Balancing Disney’s playful brand identity with the formal tone required for corporate materials was a key hurdle. To resolve this, I developed a hybrid visual style—using clean, minimalist layouts infused with subtle Disney motifs (e.g., hidden Mickeys, custom typography) to maintain professionalism without losing magic. Tight deadlines for multiple iterations were another challenge, which I addressed by creating reusable templates and asset libraries to streamline the design process.

Adapting designs for both print magazines and digital platforms demanded flexibility. I prioritized scalable vector graphics and modular layouts to ensure cross-platform consistency. Additionally, mastering Disney’s strict brand guidelines required meticulous attention to detail, solved by collaborating closely with the brand team to align every element with their standards.

Technologies Used

Adobe Illustrator InDesign

Results

  • ✅ Enhanced brand consistency across corporate and editorial materials
  • ✅ Praised for innovative layouts that balanced creativity with professionalism
